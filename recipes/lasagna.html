Arugula Salad with Stone Fruit



    This juicy, vibrant arugula salad features fresh cherries,
    peaches, and nectarines for a summery flavor. For an
    extra touch of sweetness, opt for rosé vinegar, a rosé 
    wine-based vinegar with a bright, fruity flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar or rosé vinegar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups red cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 1/2 cups yellow cherry tomatoes, halved

Steps

  1. Whisk together olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a small bowl for the dressing.

  2. Arrange tomatoes, arugula, basil, nectarines, and peach slices on a large platter.
    Drizzle with half the dressing. Top with cherries, sea salt, and remaining dressing.
    Serve immediately.
